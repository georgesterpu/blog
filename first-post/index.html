Hello

Welcome, stranger!

I am launching this blog in order to write about various topics in speech research, academia, computers, hiking, and life. One of the reasons for starting the blog is to share my knowledge in the area of speech modelling and help drive the field forward. I also hope that the blog will help me organise my thoughts better and become a better thinker and writer. I can only surmise that the blog content will get better with time.

The blog is made using a static site generator called Zola, which is written in the Rust programming language. Zola allows me to focus entirely on the content of this blog, as it requires me to write in an intermediate human-readable representation called Markdown, from which it automatically generates the web pages. Thanks to everyone contributing to the Zola framework!
